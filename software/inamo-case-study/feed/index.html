Firstly, this project predates the iPad, and such technology wasn’t available then. Only very recently have large, thin, capacitive touch screens become available. And they’re very expensive, and fragile, and still not quite big enough for a 4 person restaurant table. Secondly, even if we did use a capacitive membrane in addition to an overhead projector to make a large robust touch screen, as you relax and eat at a table you touch the table with your skin all the time.
